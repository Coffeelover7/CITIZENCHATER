Citizen Charter - Ministry of Health

Our vision: A healthier nation that contributes to its economic, social, mental and spiritual development.
Our mission: To contribute to the social and economic development of Sri Lanka by achieving the highest attainable health status through promotive, preventive, curative and rehabilitative services of high quality made available and accessible to people of Sri Lanka.
